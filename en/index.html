All about me

After graduating from high school in 2002 I started studying at the Technical University of Chemnitz, Germany. I successfully completed my studies in 2008 by receiving my diploma in computer science. Since then, I’ve been working for gocept gmbh & co. kg in Halle as a software engineer.

In my free-time I enjoy taking pictures, doing sports (e.g. I love running and spending time outside in the nature which helps me to reduce stress and stay fit, both mentally & physically; in 2008 I completed my first marathon which I’m very proud of; I also like playing soccer and badminton on a regular basis), playing computer games, watching movies, listening to music, socialising and pathing. :)
